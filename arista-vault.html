What is Wallet Bot?

Wallet-bot is a new age wallet with tech enabled features like anti lost/anti-theft, inbuilt 3000 Mah power charging, separation alarm, selfie button and it is RFID protected. It has two-way Smart connectivity with the mobile phone and wallet. which ensures the safety of your wallet as well as your mobile phone. While designing the team makes sure to keep the aesthetics in  mind. Hence, all wallets are made of Design engineered leather.
